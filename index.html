Projects

Physics Project: PN Junction Diode

As part of a group project in Physics, we developed an Earthquake Detector using a simple transistor-based circuit that made lights turn on and off alternately when vibrations were detected. Working together, we designed and assembled the circuit using transistors, resistors, and LEDs to simulate an early-warning system. This project gave us hands-on experience with circuit design, teamwork, and practical application of transistors as switches and amplifiers, while also showing how basic electronics can be applied to create cost-effective safety devices for real-life situations.

DDD Project: E-commerce and Online Shopping System

I developed an E-Commerce and Online Shopping System using MySQL, incorporating features such as customer management, product catalog, shopping cart, and order tracking. The project gave me practical experience in database design, normalization, and ER modeling, along with implementing CRUD operations, SQL joins, and stored procedures for efficient data handling. I also designed relational tables to manage customer profiles, inventory, transactions, and order histories, which helped me strengthen my skills in query optimization, data integrity, and real-world application of database systems.

Future Project: College Club and Event

We are planning to develop a digital platform for the Samtse College of Education (SCE) that will allow students and staff to register online and stay updated with important information. The platform will act as a central hub for communication, where users can easily access announcements, events, schedules, and other academic or administrative updates. By digitizing these services, the system aims to reduce paperwork, save time, and enhance accessibility, ensuring a more efficient, transparent, and user-friendly experience for the SCE community.
